In order to prepare for the next hackathon I wanted to learn a new technology and D3.js caught my interest. I made this project to prep for the event. If takes a static csv file about baseball stats and categorize them by team. You can also choose what stat to graph with the ui tool above. Hovering over a portion of the graph will show what team is represented there you can click it to zoom in.
