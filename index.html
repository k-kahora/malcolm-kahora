My keyboards of choice

  • Ferris Sweep : My first ergo board, very fun to use. The low profile makes this thing very snappy.
  • Charybdis Mini : Absolute hell to build due to the flexible PCB, but my most comfortable keyboard. The trackball makes this thing a bulldozer when it comes to getting things done on a computer.
  • Microdox : A very cramped keyboard, but using the nice!nanos for Bluetooth is what makes this the board I choose when working outside my room.
